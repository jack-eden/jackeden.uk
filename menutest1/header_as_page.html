About Me

Educated as a graphic designer I have mostly spent my career in a series of day jobs ranging from tech support in an ISP call centre, to project management at a Russel Group university. Some of it has been creative work, but not a lot of it. I want to change that and this blog is a part of the strategy; it's been long enough since my education that a clean start is my preferred approach and this is where I'll be keeping my notes.

I'd also expect to see a lot of notes on table-top RPGs and D&D, and collected ephemera.
